# We are the Interesting Company

We're the Interesting Company. Right now, we are trying to figure out what we will be completely. We're working on building our infastructure, planning our projects, and deciding how we will serve people.

Long term, we see a way to help people primarily in the area of larger Government organizations. Government website's are not very usable for the most part, and we would like to be a part of the solution.

We have a long ways to go. We're just getting started, and we hope to iterate slowly over the next couple years, then gear up into high gear once we have a clear path for the future.

Look out for more.
